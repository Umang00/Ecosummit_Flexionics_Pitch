## 4. Financial Projections (Slide 11)

> [!WARNING]
> **Major Discrepancies and Potentially "Made Up" Numbers Identified.**
> The financial projections in the pitch deck do not align with any of the internal business plans or forecasts stored in RAG SourceLM.

*   **2024 Revenue $12M**: **Verified**. Actual total revenues were ~$12.2M (Sourced from *250618_Flexionics_T2Y.pdf*).
*   **2024 EBITDA $3M**: **Discrepancy**. Actual 2024 EBITDA was reported as $1.52M in the financial summaries, significantly lower than $3M.
*   **2025 Revenue ~$20M**: **Discrepancy**. Most recent forecasts project 2025 revenue at $8.5M or $9.2M. An older (Feb 2025) aggressive projection showed $33.6M. The ~$20M figure does not match any source.
*   **2025 EBITDA ~$9M**: **Discrepancy**. Projected 2025 EBITDA is listed at roughly $1.0M - $1.17M in the documents. $9M is not supported by the sources.
*   **2027 Target Revenue $52.9M**: **Verified**. Sourced from *250214 Project Daedalus - Teaser.pdf* and multiple financial tables (RAG Source 1).
*   **2027 EBITDA $38M**: **Major Discrepancy**. The financial plans project an EBITDA of **~$20.2M** for 2027. It appears the pitch deck confused the projected **38% EBITDA margin** with an absolute dollar amount of **$38M**. Consequently, the listed margin of 72% in the pitch deck (calculated as 38/52.9) is also entirely incorrect. The actual sources show a 38% margin.
*   **CAGR 67%**: **Discrepancy**. The closest CAGR found in the documents is 64% (2024-2027). Other timelines show 54% or 136%.

Please review the financial projections on Slide 11. The $38M EBITDA for 2027 appears to be a direct mistake (confusing the 38% margin rate for absolute dollars), and the 2024/2025 figures appear overly optimistic or fabricated compared to the internal books loaded into RAG SourceLM. I recommend adjusting these numbers to match the internal financial models or be prepared to defend the discrepancy.
